How to Cook Ugali and Meat

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of maize flour (unga ya ugali)
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/2 kg beef (cut into cubes)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooking oil
  • Coriander (dhania) for garnish

Step 1: Preparing Ugali

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a sufuria until it starts bubbling.
  2. Gradually add maize flour while stirring with a wooden spoon to avoid lumps.
  3. Stir consistently until the mixture thickens and pulls from the sides of the sufuria.
  4. Let it steam for 2–3 minutes, then serve hot.

Step 2: Preparing the Meat Stew

  1. Wash and boil beef cubes in water until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, then add onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes (and garlic if using), stir and cook until the mixture softens.
  4. Add the cooked beef to the tomato-onion base. Simmer for 8–10 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and garnish with chopped coriander.

Serving Suggestion

For best results, serve hot with a side of sukuma wiki or spinach and a cold beverage. Enjoy your meal!
